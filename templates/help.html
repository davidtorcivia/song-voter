How to Use

Getting Started

Song Voter helps you rate and compare songs. Select a mode and click Begin to start listening.

Listening

Each song plays automatically. Use the controls to:

  • ▶ / ❙❙ — Play or pause
  • ⏭ — Skip to next song (no vote saved)
  • Spacebar — Toggle play/pause
  • Progress bar — Click or drag to seek

Voting

You must listen for at least 20 seconds before voting is enabled.

  • △ Keep — Mark as a song you'd keep
  • ▽ Pass — Mark as a song you'd skip
  • Rating 1-10 — Rate the song quality

Click Next → to save your vote and continue.

Results

View aggregate ratings on the Results page. Click column headers to sort.
