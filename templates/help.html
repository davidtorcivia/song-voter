How to Use

Getting Started

Song Voter helps you rate and compare different versions of songs. Perfect for choosing between mixes, masters, or variations.

Select a mode and click Begin to start:

  • All Songs — Shuffle through every song in random order
  • Specific Track — Compare all versions of a single track back-to-back

Playback Controls

Each song plays automatically. Use these controls:

  • ▶ / ❙❙ — Play or pause
  • ⏭ — Skip to next song without voting
  • Spacebar — Toggle play/pause
  • Progress bar — Click or drag to seek
  • Cast button — Stream to Chromecast or other devices

Voting

Listen for at least 20 seconds before voting is enabled.

  • △ Keep — This version is worth keeping
  • ▽ Pass — You'd skip this version
  • Rating 1-10 — Rate overall quality

Click Next → to save your vote and continue.

Results

View aggregate ratings on the Results page. Click column headers to sort and find your favorites.
